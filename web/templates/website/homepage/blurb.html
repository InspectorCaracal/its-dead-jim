The City of Nexus

A microcosm of the future, where society is ruled by corporate businesses; where magic is real, known, and tightly regulated; where anyone and everyone who doesn't fit into society norms is cast aside, ignored and forgotten.

But forgotten doesn't mean alone.

Welcome to downcity, where those "forgotten" by the corporate hegemony live and community means survival. The poor and the outcast, the rebel and the criminal; whoever or whatever you are, you'll be welcomed.
